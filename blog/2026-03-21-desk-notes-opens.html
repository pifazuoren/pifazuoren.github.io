Desk Notes Opens

2026-03-21 note archive

This is the first page in the archive. The homepage stays compact and stable; small daily material moves here instead.

The idea is simple: anything that changes often should live outside the introduction page. Short thoughts, one-day moods, loose observations, and little photo trails fit better in a running archive than in a static profile.

This page is intentionally plain. It should feel easy to update, easy to scan later, and calm enough that even tiny entries still look cared for.

Filing Rule

One post can be just a paragraph and one image. It does not need to become a full essay to deserve a place here.
