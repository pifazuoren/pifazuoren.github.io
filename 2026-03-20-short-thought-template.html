2026-03-20 text template

This is the small-entry format: one idea, one day, a few lines. No need to force completeness.

Some entries can stay unfinished on purpose. A notebook is allowed to contain fragments. In fact, the archive becomes more believable when not every piece arrives polished.

If a thought later grows into something longer, it can become a separate entry. But the short version should still be allowed to remain here, in its original shape.

Starter Prompt

  • What small thing stayed in my mind?
  • Did I photograph it, or only notice it?
